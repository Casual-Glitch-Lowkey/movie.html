Brief Description Blue Lock the Movie: Episode Nagi (2024) tells the story of Seishiro Nagi, a naturally talented but lazy high school student who discovers his passion for soccer after meeting Reo Mikage. Together they enter the intense Blue Lock training program designed to create the world's greatest striker for Japan.
